Day Planning

  • August 08, 2024
  • Planning

If you are working or still studying, day planning is a must for you. Not only for them, this is actually very useful for everyone, men and women.

Why do you need to plan your day,üí° If you plan your day, you can reduce wasting your time unnecessarily. Because you have to do today, you have another to-do list. Because of this, you can quickly switch from one work to another without being bored.

if you plan your day in advance, the bad habit of procrastination will go away from you. Because of this, you will be able to live without stress and not miss work. üí° And the happiness you get at the end of the day when all the planned work is completed. How do you plan your day?

You can make a To Do List. Some people also call it a check list. In a to do list, you write down all the work you have to do today. You can do this freely as you want. Because your routine and my routine, the things you have to do and the things I have to do are differentüôâ

After making the list, you can put a mark in front of your first task, or the first task you completed from the list. At the end of the day, if all the tasks on your list are completed, you winüëëÔ∏è

If left then,

You can put them at the top of the list the next day. It doesn't matter if it is not necessary. Just write the list saying it is like that, it is not like that

When making the list, give priority to the priorities. Then the essential work you have to do will definitely be done. For example, you have to submit an assignment this afternoon. A page of that assignment has not been done yet. But you are on the list to go to pizza hut and eat a pizza. At this time, the first thing to do is the assignment rather than eating pizza. You can eat pizza even tomorrow

Also, don't put too much work into the list. Include as much work as you can do in a day. Even if you lose idea about your work ability in the beginning, after a while you can balance the list well. Work is not only the things that strain the body but also the things that you do for yourself. So keep things like self care and meditation on the list.This will be difficult at first. It will not be possible to finish all the work. But after 21 days, you will not procrastinate, manage time well, get small achievements in life, be happy and be a beautiful person ‚ù§
