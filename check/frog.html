The Frog Story

  • August 08, 2024
  • Motivation

One day a herd of gumbo was going on a journey. Suddenly, 06 people from this herd fell into a big hole that they couldn't get out of easily. These six frogs were very tired and tried hard to get out of the hole

In the meantime, the other frogs on top of the pit shouted to the frogs inside the pit from around. " Oh Sin ", " How Sinful ", " It's Time ", " It's Karume ", " You Can't Do That ", " Who Came Out of the Oya ", " Now It's Gonna Die ", " Come on ", " Nobody in History You haven't come out of the hole."

Hearing this,

the frogs who had fallen into the hole gave up their efforts to escape from the hole and began to cry.. But one frog did not give up his efforts and kept jumping up and trying to get out of the hole. , I am a fool, even if I die, I will not die in peace" and started shouting. Two days passed.. one or two frogs in the hole started to die.. but the frog that tried to come up slowly came to the top of the hole with his efforts, and at the same time took a big leap and came to the surface..

Seeing this, the frogs left inside the pit and the frogs sitting on top were surprised. Even if all 6 frogs tried, why only one frog was able to come up. Because you keep trying? not..

What really happened here? The other frogs who were sitting on the pits were encouraging the frogs that had fallen into the pits to come up, instead of helping them, they started saying things that discouraged them. So they gave up trying to go ashore and started to grieve and cry. But that deaf frog did not hear these things. He saw only the shadows of the frogs around the pit. That's why he thought that the frog on top was clapping his hands and encouraging him to come up. The frogs inside the hole were shouting to encourage him.. So he felt strong, wanted to come out, and had courage. However, he tried harder and finally managed to save his life.

When you try to start something new, the people around you say, "Are you crazy? Are you trying to touch the moon? It doesn't matter if you have studied. Dates can do anything. What can you do here? Are you trying to lose what you have? Do you want to be ashamed?" fools do" A lot of negetive ideas like this are put inside your head. Then what happened to the other frogs in those holes will happen. Your hopes, your goals end there. Most of the time, you lose success because you believe in the people around you like the frogs around you, rather than you believe in yourself, because you are going to listen to them. Accept and follow the good things people say. But be careful. Because there are more people in today's society who try to pull you down than those who sincerely wish you well. Make a goal. Work towards the goal and believe in yourself. Enjoy the victory. I always say,,, there is no one in this world like you, like you so, Conquer your way to success you too Be a deaf frog ‚ù§
